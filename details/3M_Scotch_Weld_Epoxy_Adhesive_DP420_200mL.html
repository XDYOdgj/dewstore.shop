  The 3M Scotch-Weld Epoxy Adhesive DP420 is a high-performance two-part epoxy adhesive designed for industrial and scientific use. This versatile bonding agent offers exceptional strength and resistance to both impact and fatigue, making it suitable for applications that require a durable, reliable bond. With its off-white color and 200 mL (6.76 fl oz) capacity, it is perfect for projects demanding precision and adaptability, whether you're working with metals, ceramics, or composite materials.
  3M Scotch-Weld Epoxy Adhesive DP420 meets the needs of various industries such as transportation, metalworking, and general manufacturing. UL 94 HB certification further confirms its suitability for demanding environments.
