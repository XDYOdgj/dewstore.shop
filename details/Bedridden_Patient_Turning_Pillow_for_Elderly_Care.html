Bedridden Patient Turning Pillow for Elderly Care

USD31.79
A U-shaped turning pillow designed to help bedridden patients and elderly individuals change position easily and comfortably. Ideal for caregiving, it reduces pressure sores and muscle atrophy while being easy to clean and durable.
  • Description
  • Reviews

  The Bedridden Patient Turning Pillow is designed to make turning over easier and safer for those who are unable to move independently. Its U-shaped grooves cradle the legs, ensuring comfort during the repositioning process. The thick, high-density sponge layer offers excellent support and distributes pressure evenly, reducing the risk of pressure sores and enhancing overall comfort. Crafted with breathable PU leather, this pillow is gentle on the skin and ensures a pleasant touch without any strong odors. The included waterproof cover makes cleaning simple, and its durability ensures long-lasting use in a caregiving environment.
  This turning device is ideal for elderly care or for individuals with limited mobility, including post-surgery patients, those with paralysis, or lower limb fractures. It allows caregivers to assist in repositioning with ease, helping maintain hygiene and comfort. Whether turning over for bed care, changing clothes, or managing medical needs, the pillow provides reliable support and minimizes strain on both the caregiver and the patient. Its versatile design ensures it can be used in multiple scenarios, making it an essential tool in any care setting.
